# Welcome to Academy of Robotic Sciences (ARS)

> **Building the Next Generation of Humanoid Robotics**

## Our Mission

Academy of Robotic Sciences (ARS) is an open-source educational platform dedicated to training the next generation of robotic experts through hands-on, project-based learning. We believe that the future of humanoid robotics lies in accessible, practical education that bridges the gap between theory and real-world application.

## Your Learning Journey

Academy of Robotic Sciences (ARS) offers five specialized learning tracks, each designed to develop expertise in a critical area of humanoid robotics:

### Choose Your Path

- **[Robotic Designer](/docs/tracks/design)** - Master CAD, 3D printing, and mechanical systems
- **[Robotic Hardware Engineer](/docs/tracks/hardware)** - Build with electronics, sensors, and actuators
- **[Robotic Software Engineer](/docs/tracks/software)** - Program kinematics, motion planning, and control systems
- **[Robotic AI Engineer](/docs/tracks/ai)** - Train robots with machine learning and computer vision
- **[Robotic Systems Engineer](/docs/tracks/systems)** - Integrate complete robotic systems
