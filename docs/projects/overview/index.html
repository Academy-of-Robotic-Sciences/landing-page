Projects Overview

Robot Campus projects are designed to provide hands-on experience with real robotic systems. Each project builds on the skills from our learning tracks while teaching practical implementation.

  • Inverse kinematics
  • ROS2 integration
  • Pick-and-place tasks

Master robotic learning using the LeRobot framework. Train robots to perform complex tasks through imitation learning and reinforcement learning.

  • Model training
  • Sim-to-real transfer
  • Policy deployment

Duration: 2-4 months

  • Iterative design
