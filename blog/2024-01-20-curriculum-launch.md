## üìö The Foundation: Shared Core Courses

Every Robot Campus student begins with the same foundational knowledge through six core courses:

### RC-101: Introduction to Robotics (2 weeks)
Start your journey with the history, types, and fundamental concepts of robotics. No prerequisites required!

### RC-102: Mathematics for Robotics (3 weeks)
Master essential math including:
- Linear algebra and transformations
- Kinematics and dynamics
- Probability and statistics
- Practical implementation in Python

### RC-103: Programming Fundamentals (3 weeks)
Learn to code with a robotics focus:
- Python and C++ essentials
- ROS2 basics
- Version control with Git
- Debugging robotic systems

### RC-104: Physics & Mechanics (2 weeks)
Understand the physical principles behind robot motion:
- Forces and torques
- Energy and power
- Material properties
- Mechanical advantage

### RC-105: Electronics Fundamentals (2 weeks)
Get comfortable with circuits and components:
- Digital vs analog
- Sensors and actuators
- Power management
- Basic troubleshooting

### RC-106: Safety & Ethics (1 week)
Critical knowledge for responsible robotics:
- Lab safety protocols
- Ethical considerations
- Environmental impact
- Human-robot interaction guidelines
